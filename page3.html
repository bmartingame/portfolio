IMPERIUS

PreviousNextClose

Imperius is an upcoming sci-fi real-time strategy game by Incognito Interactive and is set to release in the Summer of 2023. As a major qualifying project in WPI's IMGD program, it won the award for Technical Excellence in May 2023. I officially joined the team in August 2022 and have been working as a game programmer and designer, working on aspects such as building and unit economy, designing and implementing of upgrades, enemy AI, all 6 campaign levels, and narrative design.
