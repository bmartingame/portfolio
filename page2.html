Background: I have a long and storied history with video games. I fell in love with the medium and its potential at a very young age and have pursued that passion ever since. I don't have much professional experience however as I am still in college but I'm proud of the work I've done through my classes. It is my dream to be able to be a part of a team that's passionate about what they're creating and the stories they are telling and to be able to create meaningful games with them. 

Prior to college I enrolled in numerous summer camps through the iD Tech program to develop my coding and game development skills and was a part of the inaugural class of their AcademyNEXT program where we spent four weeks learning about and working with AI,

I've also worked on stuff outside of video games such as volunteer work with Wolf Trap Animal Rescue, and numerous outreach programs with my High School Robotics Team, FRC Team 5549.

Beyond that, I have also worked with the Swiss Academy of Engineering to help design a multimedia narrative to promote new and upcoming technologies.
